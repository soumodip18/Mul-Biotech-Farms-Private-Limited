Core Features

Six integrated intelligence layers that convert raw geospatial and environmental data into actionable agricultural and compliance insights.

🌍
India-Wide Soil Nutrient Mapping

Granular soil health maps covering nitrogen, phosphorus, potassium, organic carbon and pH levels across agricultural zones of India. Drilldown by district, block and survey number.

🌞
Real-Time Climate Telemetry

Live weather station data including temperature, humidity, rainfall and solar radiation overlaid on the geospatial canvas. Updated continuously from multiple meteorological sources.

🛰
Satellite NDVI & Thermal Overlays

Processed satellite imagery providing vegetation indices (NDVI, EVI), land surface temperature, and change-detection layers for crop health assessment and environmental monitoring.

🌿
Crop Recommendation Engine

AI-assisted crop recommendation system that cross-references soil nutrient profiles, climate data, and local agronomic benchmarks to suggest optimal crops and input regimes.

📋
Environmental Compliance Layer

Overlay of environmental protected zones, buffer areas, and regulatory boundaries sourced from MoEFCC PARIVESH for EIA and land-use clearance workflows.

📄
Export-Ready Reports

Automated generation of soil health certificates, environmental baseline reports, and data exports in formats compatible with government submission portals and international standards.

Core Features

End-to-end digital MRV capabilities from sensor data collection through to carbon credit documentation and regulatory submission.

📈
Real-Time Pond Parameter Monitoring

Continuous tracking of dissolved oxygen, pH, temperature, ammonia, nitrite, nitrate, salinity, turbidity, and TDS across multiple ponds simultaneously via IoT sensor integration.

🧬
Biofloc & Microbial Analytics

Proprietary biofloc volume, floc quality, and microbial community health indices derived from water quality parameters. Alerts for system stress before mortality events occur.

🌿
Carbon Sequestration Accounting

Automated calculation of carbon sequestration, methane avoidance, and net GHG balance for aquaculture systems, with methodology aligned to Gold Standard and Verra AMS frameworks.

📝
Article 6.4 MRV Documentation

Structured reporting templates aligned with UNFCCC Article 6.4 requirements for international carbon market participation. Audit-trail generation for third-party verification.

📊
Regulatory Compliance Reports

Automated generation of CPCB, SPCB, and aquaculture authority compliance reports. Pre-formatted for submission to MPEDA, NFDB, and state fisheries departments.

🔑
Multi-Pond Dashboard & Alerts

Unified dashboard for up to 50 ponds with real-time alert system for parameter breaches, feeding schedules, harvest readiness indicators, and pond-to-pond comparison analytics.
