Who We Are

Applied Rural Biotech.
Systems-First Thinking.

Mul Biotech Farms Private Limited is an applied agri-biotech company based in Rishra, Hooghly, West Bengal — incorporated in 2026 but operating since 2019, where twelve integrated biological systems ran in parallel across aquaponics, biofloc, hydroponics, agroforestry, spirulina and mushroom cultivation.

“The IRBAS™ framework did not emerge from a design table. It emerged from six years of watching biological systems fail and recover — including the day 7,000 climbing perch died and forced us to understand nitrogen cycles at a systems level.”

  • MissionTo design and implement regenerative biological systems that restore ecosystem function while generating livelihoods.
  • VisionA rural India where biotechnology and environmental intelligence are accessible public infrastructure.
  • PhilosophySystems before outputs. Measurement before claims. Communities before projects.

Research & Innovation

The IRBAS™ Framework & Environmental Intelligence

A proprietary integrated systems architecture developed from six years of field observation and 29+ research publications.

IRBAS™
Integrated Regenerative
Biosphere Architecture System

A Systems Architecture for Regenerative Landscapes

IRBAS™ is not a product or a service — it is a framework for designing, implementing and measuring integrated biological systems at landscape scale. It emerged not from a design process but from field observation: tracking the failure and recovery of twelve simultaneous biological systems at Unit Nasibpur from 2019 to 2024.

The framework is structured into six operational stacks, each addressing a distinct layer of the regenerative systems problem — from ecological restoration to digital verification.

IRBAS-RESTORE™

Ecological restoration, soil health regeneration and degraded land recovery protocols.

IRBAS-BIOENERGY™

Biomass, biogas and decentralised bioenergy systems for rural communities.

IRBAS-HYDROGRID™

Integrated water management: aquaponics, RAS, biofloc and water quality architecture.

IRBAS-CARBON™

Carbon sequestration design, project documentation and Article 6.4-aligned MRV.

IRBAS-GEOSPATIAL™

GIS, remote sensing, environmental dashboards and spatial intelligence platforms.

IRBAS-RURAL™

Farmer training, FPO capacity building and rural enterprise development systems.
