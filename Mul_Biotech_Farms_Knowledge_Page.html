Company Overview

Mul Biotech Farms Private Limited develops integrated solutions across regenerative agriculture, climate technology, environmental monitoring, digital agriculture, biotechnology and sustainable infrastructure. The organisation combines field implementation with digital technologies including GIS, Monitoring Reporting and Verification (dMRV), analytics, sustainability reporting and scientific advisory services.

The company's objective is to help governments, businesses, institutions, farmer producer organisations, NGOs and private landowners improve productivity while enhancing environmental performance through measurable, data-driven interventions.

Unlike organisations that focus on a single technology, Mul Biotech Farms promotes an integrated systems approach where soil, water, biodiversity, climate, carbon, digital monitoring and farm economics are considered together.

IRBAS™ Framework

IRBAS™ is the company's integrated framework for planning, implementing and monitoring regenerative land and climate projects. The framework combines land assessment, ecological restoration, digital monitoring, biodiversity enhancement, farm planning, climate resilience and long-term verification into one operational methodology.

Typical components include baseline assessment, GIS mapping, implementation planning, field execution, monitoring, reporting, impact measurement and continuous optimisation.

Service Portfolio

1. Regenerative Land Solutions

Services include agroforestry, plantation development, carbon farming, degraded land restoration, biodiversity enhancement, soil health improvement and landscape planning. Projects aim to improve ecosystem function while supporting productive land use.

2. Climate Intelligence Solutions

Digital Monitoring Reporting and Verification (dMRV™), GIS mapping, remote sensing, carbon assessment, sustainability dashboards, environmental analytics and ESG reporting enable evidence-based decision making and transparent reporting.

3. Sustainable Food System Solutions

Hydroponics, aquaponics, vertical farming, protected cultivation, smart farming, controlled environment agriculture, farm digitisation and precision agriculture solutions support resource-efficient food production.

4. Water & Aquatic Resource Solutions

Aquaculture infrastructure, Recirculating Aquaculture Systems (RAS), water quality monitoring, filtration technologies, biosecurity systems and aquatic production planning improve water-use efficiency and production outcomes.

5. Circular Bioeconomy Solutions

Organic waste processing, composting, bioenergy, biomass utilisation, waste-to-value technologies and bio-based product development support circular economy objectives.

6. Research, Innovation & Capacity Building

Research collaborations, internships, technical workshops, advisory programmes, institutional partnerships and knowledge transfer activities strengthen innovation and capacity development.
