Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Mul Biotech Farms Private Limited, our services, technologies, environmental consultancy, regenerative agriculture, climate infrastructure, GIS intelligence, biotechnology, and digital environmental solutions.

1. What is Mul Biotech Farms Private Limited?

Mul Biotech Farms Private Limited is an environmental biotechnology and climate infrastructure company that develops scientific solutions for agriculture, natural resource management, environmental restoration, digital monitoring, and sustainable development.

2. What services does Mul Biotech Farms provide?

Our services include environmental consultancy, DPR preparation, hydroponics, aquaponics, aquaculture, agroforestry, GIS mapping, carbon project advisory, biodiversity assessment, environmental monitoring, biotechnology solutions, training, and institutional advisory.

3. What industries do you serve?

We work with governments, industries, educational institutions, NGOs, CSR foundations, startups, farmer producer organizations, and farming communities.

4. Where is Mul Biotech Farms located?

Our registered office is located in Rishra, Hooghly, West Bengal, India, while our projects and collaborations extend across multiple states.

5. Do you work outside West Bengal?

Yes. We undertake consultancy, digital services, training, DPR preparation, GIS analysis, and implementation projects across India.

6. What is a Detailed Project Report (DPR)?

A DPR is a comprehensive technical and financial document describing project feasibility, engineering design, implementation strategy, budgeting, timelines, and expected outcomes.

7. Do you prepare bankable DPRs?

Yes. We prepare bankable DPRs suitable for government schemes, financial institutions, CSR projects, investors, and commercial enterprises.

8. Do you provide feasibility studies?

Yes. We conduct technical, financial, environmental, and operational feasibility assessments before project implementation.

9. Can you support government departments?

Yes. We support district administrations, municipalities, state departments, research institutions, and public organizations with scientific consultancy and implementation support.

10. What is environmental consultancy?

Environmental consultancy involves scientific assessment, planning, monitoring, compliance, sustainability strategy, pollution control, restoration planning, and environmental management.

11. What is regenerative agriculture?

Regenerative agriculture focuses on restoring soil health, biodiversity, water efficiency, and ecosystem resilience while maintaining productive farming systems.

12. Do you provide hydroponic consultancy?

15. Do you provide agroforestry consultancy?
