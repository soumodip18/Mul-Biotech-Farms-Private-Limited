About the Repository

The Mul Biotech Farms Knowledge Repository serves as a centralized archive of publications, technical papers, climate intelligence frameworks, case studies, workshop reports, dashboard documentation, and agricultural research outputs.

This repository reflects the organization’s work across regenerative agriculture, aquaculture, climate adaptation, carbon monitoring, GIS systems, environmental intelligence, and digital agriculture.
